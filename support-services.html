Our Support Package

Extensive Support

Once we have established that you are a perfect fit for us and vice versa, Looks Salon Franchisees benefit from an extensive support package.

Salon Design

We make recommendations for a host of talented design houses that have years of experience in creating cutting edge, bespoke salons for Looks Salon and will guide you from planning to completion.

Regional Business Manager

The Regional Business Manager's aim is to fully support each franchisee and their business, creating the Perfect Salon in each location.

Education

With a philosophy of motivation and inspiration, our education programme is seen as fundamental to the brand's success. We have our own Academy and endless creative and business education for your ongoing development.

Human Resources and Customer Service

Our dedicated Human Resources team create the blueprint for recruitment and selection, interviewing techniques, company handbook and policies and job descriptions. They work alongside our employment law agency to support and cover you. Our customer services team are on hand to step in when you can't resolve a customer complaint and help you to reach an ideal outcome.

Salon Genius Computer Systems

We provide a fully supported business management system built to develop your business and keep you connected with all your customers.

Our Partner Brands

The Exclusive Unisex Salon in your town acquainted with world-class tools and professionals for stunning looks and absolute luxury.
